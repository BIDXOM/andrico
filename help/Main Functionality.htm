Main Functionality

With Andrico you can:

  • Connect to Facebook server using our application firstly and getting Authorization Token Key - no more entering password and login required in further
  • Get information from your Facebook contacts and synchronizing it with local database on your device
  • Search your friends on a database directly on your device without Internet Connection and viewing information about their locations, birthdays, events and userpics
  • Go to your friends profile pages on Facebook shortly and viewing where are their locations on the Google Maps
  • Show members of events and show the location of event on Google Maps
  • Filter your Search requests in local database
  • Setting up your preferences to load just general information to reduce internet traffic, or to enhance speed of synchronization in a low-banded internet connection
  • Merge database of your contacts with any other user who use our application from the one device
