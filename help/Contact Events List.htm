Contact Events List

Contact Events List represent all of events in which contact takes part. You will see the Title of event and date and time of performance.

You can scroll this list by a finger ore use hardware jostick to choose the contact your need. Also you can type first letters of your contact on your keybord to make search faster.

Also you can use filter by clicking on a hardware button "menu" and to choose "SHOW ALL EVENTS" checkbox to filter All events in which choosen contacts takes part and User of Andrico takes part, or "SHOW MY EVENTS" to view events in which you only takes part.

You can just click on a event, and get more information about it in a Contact Event window.

If there are no events in which choosen contact or you takes part, you will see an information dialog "NO EVENTS AVALIABLE":

  • Clicking by a first button "please,SYNCHRONIZE, at first" will follow you to Synchronization menu, wher you can relogin or gettin an Authorization Token
  • Clicking by a "BACK TO MENU" wil follow you to Main menu of Andrico.
