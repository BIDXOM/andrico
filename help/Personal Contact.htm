Personal Contact

Personal Contact window represents information of a choosen contact, getted from facebook server.

Clicking by "CONTACT EVENTS" follow you to all Contact Facebook Events List in which contact takes part

Clicking by "SHOW ADDRESS ON MAP" follow you to a Location of a contact on a Google Maps

Clicking by "GO TO FACEBOOK PAGE" follow you to a Contact Profile Page on a Facebook

 

Some of fields may be empty by a different reasons:

You have disabled option "Synchronize Pictures" in Settings and you cannot see his userpick. In this case there is no avatar of User

Synchronized Contact restricted access to his birthday, location or event information. In this case text fields will be filled with a title

"no adress avaliable" or "no birthday available" and Buttons will be disabled
